About ProJenius:

ProJenius, also written as Projenius on the website, is a technology-driven startup and innovation technology company.

The company focuses on building smart digital solutions using AI, IoT, software development, web development, mobile applications, product engineering, UI/UX design, and practical technology.

The company works with businesses, startups, industries, and students to create impactful digital products and solve real-world problems through creativity, technology, and innovation.
